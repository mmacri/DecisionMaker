Certification Mode (Fast Track)

Linear modules, knowledge checks per topic, and a wrap-up exam that issues a certificate.

  • Seven concise modules with guided summaries.
  • Locked sequence keeps you focused on the exam path.
  • Passing score unlocks the downloadable certificate.

Interactive Mode (Deep Dive)

Full runtime experience with additional drills, manual completion gating, and scenario-rich prompts.

  • Embeds the full fifteen-step runtime.
  • Manual “done” controls keep modules orderly.
  • Switch back to the fast track anytime.

How this works

2. Complete modules

3. Pass exam

4. Download certificate

Capture your name, role, and completion details.
